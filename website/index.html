What is Kids Curiosity Club?

Kids Curiosity Club is an AI-powered podcast network designed to ignite the imagination and curiosity of children ages 6-12. Our mission is to make learning an adventure—one story, one question, and one discovery at a time.

Each week, our lovable hosts—like Hannah the Historian and Oliver the Inventor—take listeners on journeys through science, history, and the wonders of the world. Every episode is crafted by educators and storytellers to be both fun and fact-filled, helping kids (and their parents!) explore new ideas together.
