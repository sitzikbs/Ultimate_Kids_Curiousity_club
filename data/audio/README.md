# Audio Assets

This directory contains audio assets for podcast production.

## Structure

- `music/` - Background music tracks (royalty-free)
- `branding/` - Intro, outro, and branding audio files

## Music Tracks

Background music should be:
- Royalty-free or licensed for podcast use
- Loopable (seamless transitions)
- Appropriate for educational content (upbeat, positive)
- 120-140 BPM recommended

## Branding Audio

- `intro.mp3` - Podcast intro (5-10 seconds)
- `outro.mp3` - Podcast outro (5-10 seconds)
- `album_art.png` - Default podcast album art (1400x1400 or 3000x3000)

## Usage

These assets are loaded by the AudioMixer service during episode production.
