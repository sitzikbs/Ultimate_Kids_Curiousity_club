# Branding Audio

This directory contains intro, outro, and branding audio files.

## Files

- `intro.mp3` - Podcast intro (5-10 seconds)
- `outro.mp3` - Podcast outro (5-10 seconds)
- `album_art.png` - Podcast album art (1400x1400 or 3000x3000 pixels)

## Intro/Outro Guidelines

Intro and outro should:
- Be short (5-10 seconds)
- Include podcast name/branding
- Set the tone for the show
- Be consistent across episodes

## Album Art Guidelines

Album art should:
- Be square (1400x1400 minimum, 3000x3000 recommended)
- PNG or JPEG format
- Include podcast logo/title
- Be colorful and kid-friendly
- Follow Apple Podcasts guidelines

## Creating Custom Branding

You can customize intro/outro for specific episodes by providing custom files
in the episode metadata, otherwise these defaults will be used.
